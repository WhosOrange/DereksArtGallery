The 10 Best Jojo's Bizarre Adventure Characters

Number 10: Hol Horse



What was once a goofy minor antagonist become an unassuming protagonist in one of the most underated pieces of Jojo's Bizarre Adventure media, Crazy Diamond's Demonic Heartbreak. I love his character progression from Stardust into the Diamond prequel. He has surprisingly great chemistry with the Higashgitas and the team up with Boingo is pretty fun.

Number 9: Johnny Joestar



Johnny doesn't need to walk to stand proud. He rides a horse. Horses are pretty rad. He also got his stand while in Arizona.

Number 8: Robert E.O. Speedwagon



He never married, he's just like me. Mortal Kombat should've had a cross over game with Jojo's Bizarre Adventure instead of DC, just so I could see this guy interact with one of Kung Lao's ancestors. Like, imagine the crossover appeal. That would be a solid T-rated game and Midway could have pulled it off. It would also get Capcom to collaborate with the biggest fighting game rival Midway, which never happened and it hurts my soul to this day. Can you imagine a Poker Kombat minigame with D'arby?

Number 7: Jonathan Joestar



Jonathan Joestar such a gentleman it hurts my soul. While more achievable in a more Jojolion style, I find him very aspirationonal and I want to look like him. He makes me want to play rugby. WHile 7, seems low, in my mind, 7 is another number 1. It's just lucky.

Number 6: Jean Pierre Polnereff



C'est le vie, madommouselle. Si vou play, dix nuef. Oui oui, les toilette. Gojira, around the world, daftendirekt. I am ley francios. He's my main in Tekken.

Number 5: Okyasu Nijimura



OI JOSUKE, I MADE IT TO NUMBER 5 ON THIS LIST! ISN'T THAT WHACKY?

Number 4: Guido Mista



Guido Mista is the 4th best character. He is a pretty normal guy with the one exception that he has to 6 stands instead of just one. The stands kick around bullets like its Pro Evolution Soccer, which is pretty rad. I'm glad he made it out alive, he was almost done for.

Number 3: Ermes Costello



Ermes Costello is the only character in Stone Ocean that doesn't make me cringe. She has the best attitude towards life. She looks like she'd be the seventh member of Korn (note that Korn only had 5 member at a time, with Ray being the 6th). Therefore, while Araki may have intended to name her stand, Kiss, after that one band from the Family Guy Christmas episodes, in my head she's probably more likely to have named it after the Korn song from their Atticus Ross produced untitled album. However, her name is based off of Elvis Costello. While more known to normies as some old classic rock guy, the more cultured of us (those that watched Austin Powers: The Spy Who Shagged Me) may recognize him from his collaborations with one Mr. Burt Bacharach, my favorite composer and probably the single most influencial songwriter of popular music. I love Tex-Mex, so her family's restaraunt would have probably been amazing.

Number 2: Josuke Higashgita



Josuke Higashgita is a pillar of his community. He loves his family (even when they don't know he exists). He befriends weirdos. He has an Nintendo 64. And on top of it all? Nice hair!

Number 1: Joeseph Joestar



How could he not be number one? He sounds just like him. I'm very biased, because I met both of his English voices. I was wearing a Pink Floyd shirt and his older voice started singing "Another Brick In The Wall, Pt.2". Joeseph Joestar is also cool because he doesn't need a stand beat ass. Just sunlight power. He begins his part by fighting racism then impregnates a Japanese woman. He's also dad of the year even if he didn't know Josuke existed.
