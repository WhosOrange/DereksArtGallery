Welcome

To The Notes of September 24th, 2024.




This is the story of some lost projects!

The iPad EP

I borrowed a friend's iPad for the night while staying over.
I believe it was for a Korg virtual synthisizer expressly made for the iPad but I could be wrong. I made 7 tracks of music that night, saving them all as PCM wavs.
However, the music made on it got deleted by its owner when I declined a roadtrip up to the woods. Boy was that guy angry...and petty.


Lost Super Early Music

Some of my earliest music (for better or worse) ended up getting wiped from my computer following a system restore.
I'm sad but the loss was probably not that severe.
Recording primarily consited of me with a FirstAct acoustic guitar or some music concrete made with Adobe Premiere.
Such classics as "You and Me in the Park", "Plain to Be Me", "Burn The House Right Down" and "I Don't Care"...all gone.


Anyway.


Video Projects

Along with that early music, their music videos were also lost. These included "Fundamentals", "Ur All Alone", "I Don't Care" and others.
They were (almost) all Premiere projects.
With the exception of one which was made using Windows Movie Maker on a Windows Vista.
One of the more experimental tracks: "War" had an animated music video.
It was lost from 2008 to 2023 when a family member happened to stumble upon it one day.

Currently at the moment, a sketch like series of videos dubbed "DST" is currently incomplete and spread across different computers.
In order to get a good chunk of the series, I would have to search for old drives that may not work anymore.
It went up to 3 whole seasons, a partial movie and an ultimately unfinished 4th season.
It was a shame because the 4th season had the most amount of effort.
They included lots of deliberately written and recorded material, whereas older seasons consits of manipulated home videos or odd animations.
The 4th season also featured one of my favorite sketches: Scorpion versus the Peanut Butter Jar.


AM Radio

Well, this one didn't last more than a day.
I consisted of combining two Snap Circuit projects that were shown off in the manual: an AM Radio and an AM Radio Station.
The sound quality was pretty...quality. Still, my mind was blown when I heard the distorted tunes from the station.
I wish I had taken a picture of it. Though, it was a project early enough to predate the prevelance of cell phone cameras. It was probably one of the coolest things I've done as an elementary school project.


G.I. Joe

Using a very cheap video camera I got for Christmas and Windows Movie Maker, I made a somewhat serious G.I. Joe something or whatever. It had an evil raccoon.




Parts I Wish Were In SSBU

Customized Special Moves

They are cool and can enhance the character for someone who usually dislikes them.


Jungle Hijinx and multi-layered stages

It's a shame that Jungle Hijinx is the only one. I wished there was more.


Classic in the vein of the 1st three games

This wager thing is stupid and annoying.


Twilight Princess Representation

Like just make them alternative skins for the three characters.


Board The Platforms

This is a PLATFORM fighter after all. Some people just only want to be Lil' Mac players (but for all the characters).


Cutscene and Platformer style Adventure Mode

Either small like Melee's or a significant change of gameplay like Brawl's.
World of Light is effectively just lots of Event matches from previous games. The spirit board just duplicates that content.
Which is a further downgrade from Brawl, because you can replay any event or adventure map anytime you want. Spirit Board takes that away from you in favor of randomness.


Trophies

Or at least add a description to the glorified PNGs.
They don't even tell you what games they are from. Like, how is little Timmy going to know which Fire Emblem game any given third of the roster is from?
Such a downgrade.

My ideal solution would be to just have the Stickers be for inanimate objects or stages with all of there info.
Then, trophies would be more like the Characters mode from N64.
They bothered rigging some of the trophies in Melee. DLC trophies would be hype.


Significant Decloning

Why Brawl's roster worked well. A lot of the cuts were clones (Roy, Dr. Mario, Pichu) or overrepresented (Gen 1 Pokemon). They need to give Ganondorf an actual moveset still.


Character Specific Target Tests

Brawl's greastest weakness is having 5 difficulty based Target Test instead of the previous game's character centered ones. Though, Brawl has the excuse of needing room for Subspace stages. Later games...what's your excuse.


Adding in the Cut (and sometimes better) Stages

Mute City (Melee), Jungle Hijinx, Pac-Maze, Orbital Gate Assault...bruh, just add the missing stages back in via DLC or update patch. It's not too late. Make Sector Z the secret alternate for of Corneria like the Boxing Ring.


Certain original tracks

The "Theme of Solid Snake"'s original MSX recording added a very sweet ambience to Shadow Moses kind of like MGS1's "Cavern". The lack of "Great Sea" or "Midna's Lament" is atrocious.
